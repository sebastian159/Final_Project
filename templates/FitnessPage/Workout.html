Workout Guide




Use this site as a guide to help plan your Workout!


We will discuss the 6 main muscle groups of the body

Arms

Strong arms can help us carry or lift heavy things such as a spare tire or your future kid!.

  • Bicep curls
  • Tricep Extensions
  • Shoulder Press
Legs

Needless to say, we use our legs to get us around where we need to go, they help us so we should help them!

  • Squats
  • Alternating Lunges
  • Deadlifts
Chest

Ever had a hard time bringing all of your groceries in at once? Having a strong chest will help with that :).

  • Chest-Press
  • Chest-Flys
  • Push-Ups


Back

A strong back can prove beneficial for your overall posture not to mention your back muscles are the second biggest and strongest in the body

  • Lat Pull-Downs
  • Lawnmowers
  • Lateral Rows
Abs

Developing your abs should not just be to impress someone you like but also to improve your posture and prevent lower back pain.

  • Bicycle Crunches
  • Plank
  • Leg-raises
Cardio

Cardio is a form of Aerobic exercise and can help improve endurance and overall cardiovascular health

  • Jump-Rope
  • Treadmill
  • Stairs
